FULL COURSE MEAL

appetizer. Steak & Blue Cheese Bruschetta with Onion & Roasted Tomato Jam

5 tablespoons olive oil, divided 1 large sweet onion, halved and thinly sliced 1 cup grape tomatoes, halved 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, divided 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper, divided 6 ounces cream cheese, softened 3/4 cup crumbled blue cheese 3 garlic cloves, minced 16 slices French bread baguette (1/2 inch thick) 2 beef ribeye steaks (3/4 inch thick and 8 ounces each) 1-1/2 teaspoons Montreal steak seasoning 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
