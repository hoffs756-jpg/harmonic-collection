Cheese Course

Charcuterie Boards are such a fun and beautiful way to enjoy a delicious snack or appetizer!

Ingredients

  • 3 to 5 Hard cheese ex cheddar, swiss, gouda
  • 3 to 5 Soft cheese ex brie, goat, burrata
  • Cured meats ex prosciutto, salami, chorizo
  • Nuts ex almonds, candied pecans, pistachios
  • Briny, pickled or marinated ex olives, cornchons, pickles
  • Savory dips and spreads ex whole ground mustard, hummus, balsamic dip
  • Cold cut veggies, fresh fruit and berries
  • Dried fruit, sweet spreads, dark chocolate
  • Crackers ex pita, whole grain, croccantini, toasted baguettes

Instructions

  1. Choose your board depending on your party size
  2. Start with the cheese as it anchors the board. Work in odd numbers, so, depending on the size of your party, choose 3, 5, or 7 types of cheeses to place around the board.
  3. Fold and add the meats. fold them in half and fan them out like a dec of cards, or folding them in half twice, so they are easy to grab. For thin meat (like prosciutto) fold them in a light and airy ribbon. Watch my video for visuals on how to fold cured meats before placing them on the board.
  4. Add savory and sweet accompaniments. Separate colors in a way that allows the brightest colors to stand out and place foods together that pair well together.
  5. Fill in extra space with crackers, and then any extra gaps with nuts or fruit.
