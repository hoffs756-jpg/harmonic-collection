Second Course Sardinian Goat

The use of goats milk butter makes the meat even more tender and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Goat 1,8 kg
  • Vinegar 200 ml
  • Goat butter 60g
  • Salt, pepper 10g
  • Juniper grains 10g
  • Rosemary 12g
  • Sage 12g
  • Bay leaves 10g
  • Olive oil 50ml

Instructions

  1. To prepare Sardinian goat, start by carefully washing the goat in water and vinegar. Dry it, brush it liberally with oil and wrap it in film to marinate for at least 10 hours.
  2. Chop and mix the juniper, rosemary, sage and bay, then add ground rock salt and black pepper. Use a sharp knife to make some cuts in the meat, then fill them with the aromatic mixture you have just prepared.
  3. Place the goat on a greased baking tray and spread butter all over its surface, then bake in the pre-heated oven at 200Â° for one hour.
  4. Midway through cooking, open the oven and wet the goat with some of its own gravy to ensure the meat remains tender.
  5. After one hour, remove the goat from the oven, let it cool for 5 minutes, then cut it into pieces and serve.
