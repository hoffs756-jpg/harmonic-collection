Ginger Turmeric Herbal Tea

This warming, anti-inflammatory tea blends ginger, turmeric, and cinnamon!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • half teaspoon ground turmeric
  • half teaspoon chopped fresh ginger
  • half teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 lemon wedge

Instructions

  1. gather ingredients
  2. bring water to a boil in a small saucepan, add turmeric, ginger, and cinnamon
  3. reduce heat to medium low and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. strain tea into a large glass
  5. stir in honey and serve with lemon wedge
