Entree. Beef Wellington

Rich, classic, and comforting.

Go to Entry 4 ✿

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds beef tenderloin
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened, divided
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 ounces liver paté
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 large egg yolk, beaten
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons red wine

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place beef tenderloin in a baking dish; spread 2 tablespoons softened butter over beef.
  3. Bake 10–15 minutes until browned. Reserve juices and cool beef.
  4. Increase oven to 450°F (230°C).
  5. Sauté onion + mushrooms in butter until softened; cool.
  6. Mix paté with remaining butter, season, and spread over beef; add mushroom mixture.
  7. Roll out puff pastry to ¼ inch thick and place beef in center.
  8. Fold pastry, seal edges, cut small slits, brush with egg yolk.
  9. Bake 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 425°F and bake 10–15 minutes more until golden.
  10. Boil pan juices with beef broth + red wine for 5 minutes. Serve with beef.
