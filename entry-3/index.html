Beef Wellington

A classic dish featuring beef tenderloin wrapped in mushroom duxelles and puff pastry.

Ingredients

For the Beef:

  • 2 lbs beef tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

For the Mushroom Mixture:

  • 1 lb mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh thyme

For Assembly:

  • 6-8 slices prosciutto
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper. Sear in hot oil until browned on all sides. Brush with mustard and let cool.
  2. Cook shallots and garlic in butter. Add mushrooms and cook until all liquid evaporates. Let cool.
  3. Lay out prosciutto slices. Spread mushroom mixture on top. Place beef in center and roll tightly. Refrigerate 15 minutes.
  4. Roll out puff pastry. Wrap around beef and seal with egg wash.
  5. Brush with egg wash. Bake at 425Â°F for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Info

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 4-6
