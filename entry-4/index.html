Palate Cleanser Lime Sorbet

A refreshing citrus treat

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lime zest
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring water, sugar, and lime juice to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature, about 1 hour.
  3. Stir lime zest into sorbet mixture; chill at least 2 hours.
  4. Churn in ice cream maker; freeze until ready to serve.
