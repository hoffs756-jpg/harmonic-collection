Palate Cleanser Lime Sorbet

A refreshing citrus treat

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lime zest
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, bring the water, sugar, and lime juice to a simmer. Stir constantly to help the sugar dissolve.
  2. Once all of the sugar has dissolved, remove the mixture from the heat and cool it to room temperature, about 1 hour.
  3. Stir the lime zest into the sorbet mixture. Chill the mixture for at least 2 hours.
  4. Pour the chilled sorbet base into your ice-cream maker and churn it according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Once churned, transfer it to a freezer-safe container and freeze until it's time to serve.
